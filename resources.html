Troubleshooting

Buffering Issues

Solution: Check your internet connection speed. We recommend at least 15Mbps for HD streaming. Try reducing video quality in player settings or connect via Ethernet instead of WiFi.

Channels Not Loading

Solution: Restart your device and router. Ensure your subscription is active. If problem persists, clear cache of your IPTV player or try a different player app.

EPG Not Showing

Solution: Refresh EPG data in your player settings. Ensure you\"re using the correct EPG URL provided in your account dashboard. Update your IPTV player to the latest version.

Login Issues

Solution: Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" feature. Ensure you\"re using the correct username and password combination. Contact support if locked out.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use NextView IPTV on multiple devices?

Yes, depending on your subscription plan. Our 3-month plan allows 3 simultaneous connections, 6-month plan allows 5, and 12-month plan allows up to 8 devices at the same time.

Do you offer PPV events?

Yes, we offer all major Pay-Per-View events including boxing matches, UFC fights, and wrestling events at no additional cost to our premium subscribers.

How often is your channel list updated?

We update our channel list weekly, adding new channels and removing those that are no longer available. We also take customer requests for specific channels.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No, there are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time, though we don\"t offer refunds for unused portions of your subscription period.
